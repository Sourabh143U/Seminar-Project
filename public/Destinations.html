Western Ghats

The Western Ghats are a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a mountain range that runs parallel to the western coast of India, covering the states of Maharashtra, Goa, Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, and Kerala. Matheran, Mahabaleshwar, and Lonavala are popular hill stations located in the Western Ghats known for their scenic beauty and pleasant climate.
